Where Lima Meets Istanbul

Welcome to Mezze Mio, a modern Turkish-Peruvian fusion restaurant that brings together the bold flavors of Peru and the rich culinary traditions of Türkiye. Our restaurant is designed for young adults who enjoy stylish dining, creative food, and a lively social atmosphere

From fresh ceviche of the Andes to grilled meats, vibrant sauces, and colorful mezze-style plates of Anatolia, Mezze Mio offers a unique fusion experience that celebrates two cultures in one exciting menu..

Signature Dishes

Our menu features original fusion dishes inspired by both Turkish and Peruvian cuisine, including seafood, grilled meats, spices, herbs, and colorful presentations.

Modern Atmosphere

Mezze Mio is imagined as a trendy and welcoming restaurant with music, warm lighting, and a modern design that appeals to people of all ages and social diners.

Peruvian Influence

Peruvian cuisine inspires the restaurant through fresh ingredients, citrus flavors, peppers, and a strong tradition of creative and vibrant cooking.

Turkish Influence

Turkish cuisine brings mezze culture, grilled specialties, aromatic spices, and a tradition of sharing food with family and friends.
