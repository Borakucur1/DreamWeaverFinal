ABOUT MEZZE MIO

A Turkish-Peruvian fusion restaurant inspired by culture, passion, and hospitality

About

Mezze Mio was inspired by our own story. I am Turkish and my girlfriend is Peruvian, and together we wanted to create something that reflects both of our backgrounds, traditions, and love for food. From the beginning, we saw how naturally these two cultures could come together through flavor, hospitality, and shared dining.

Living in New York City, we were inspired by the diversity around us and by the strong communities that already exist here, including around 40,000 Peruvians and 41,000 Turkish people. That made us believe even more in the potential of a restaurant that celebrates both cultures in a fresh, modern, and exciting way.

Mezze Mio is our vision of a top-quality fusion restaurant built on passion, creativity, and service. We want every guest to experience not only amazing Turkish-Peruvian cuisine, but also the warmth, care, and high level of hospitality that define both of our cultures.

OUR STORY

Inspired by our Turkish and Peruvian backgrounds, Mezze Mio brings two rich cultures together through exceptional food, warm hospitality, and a shared passion for quality.

OUR IDEA

Mezze Mio was created from our shared dream of building a restaurant that combines the best of Turkish and Peruvian cuisine. We wanted to create a place that feels personal, modern, and meaningful while introducing guests to a unique fusion of flavors and cultures.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to deliver top-quality food, excellent service, and a memorable dining experience. Mezze Mio is not only about fusion cuisine, but about honoring our roots and sharing them with New York City in a fresh and welcoming way.
